Change a simple roadway property¶

project: 6th St E Road Diet
category: Roadway Property Change
tags:
   - 'example'
dependencies:
  prerequisites: []
  corequisites: []
  conflicts: []
facility:
   link:
     - osm_link_id:
        - '223371529' # 6th St E
   A:
     osm_node_id: '187854529' # Jackson St
   B:
     osm_node_id: '187899923' # Robert St N
properties:
  - property: lanes  #changes number of lanes 3 to 2 (reduction of 1)
    existing: 3
    change: -1
notes: Simple project example to do a road diet on 6th St E in front of MetCouncil
